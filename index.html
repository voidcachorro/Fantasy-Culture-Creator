Fertile spots in desert regions with reliable water sources. Cultures develop sophisticated irrigation, date cultivation, and serve as vital trade centers.
🌊 Delta
River deltas where rivers meet the sea. Cultures develop advanced agriculture, fishing industries, and become major trade hubs due to strategic location.
🏞️ Canyon

🌋 Caldera

🏔️ Highlands
Elevated plateaus with cooler climates. Cultures develop terrace farming, unique architecture, and serve as strategic defensive positions.
🌊 Fjord

Buildings constructed on hillsides with stepped or tiered platforms. Creates usable agricultural space and distinctive silhouettes against landscapes.

Dwellings built along or over waterways with integrated canal systems. Often features bridges, water gates, and boat access to homes.

Residences built around central open courtyards that provide light, ventilation, and private outdoor space. Common in warm climates.

Simple, often round dwellings made from locally available materials like wood, thatch, or animal hides. Easy to construct and maintain.
⛺ Nomadic Tents
Portable dwellings made from fabric or hides stretched over frames. Designed for quick assembly and disassembly by mobile peoples.
🏠 Longhouses
Elongated structures that house multiple families or an entire clan. Features shared hearths and communal living spaces.
🪨 Stone Circular Homes
Round stone buildings with conical roofs. Often features intricate stonework and defensive considerations in their design.
